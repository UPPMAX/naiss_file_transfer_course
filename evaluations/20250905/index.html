    About the success score

    The shown success score of 70% is an underestimate of the actual success: in the last session, learners could work on 1 out of 4 learning outcomes. This means that a successfull lesson would entail a learner having 5 out of 8 learning outcomes done. Correcting that, by dividing 70% by (5/8) however, results in a success score of over 100%. Due to this, there is no adjustment done
